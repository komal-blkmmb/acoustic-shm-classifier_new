Step 03 / 06
Inspect Extracted Hits
Select an iteration and cell to see all hits overlaid on the same axis. Good extraction = consistent shapes. The mean ± std band shows consistency. Red-outlined hits differ significantly — possible echo artefacts or noise triggers. You can also inspect PSD and MFCC for any selected hit.
Step 04 / 06
Train Classifiers
Dataset is shuffled and split 70/30. Both sets come from the same recording sessions, so validation accuracy is optimistic — the real unseen test happens in Step 5.
